About GreenInfinity

Driven by innovation and a commitment to excellence, GreenInfinity is a leading name in the wood panel industry, crafting sustainable and high-quality solutions for modern living.

Progressing Sustainably

Established in 1994, Greenply Industries Limited (GIL) is a leading Indian interior infrastructure brand, producing superior quality plywood and allied products, decorative veneers, and Medium Density Fibre (MDF) boards. One of the largest interior infrastructure brands in India. At Greenply Industries Limited, we are deeply committed to harness the power of technology and embrace innovative manufacturing practices. These advances allow us to deliver a portfolio of products that stand apart in quality and craftsmanship, continually exceeding the expectations of our customers. Along with a strong emphasis on progress, we constantly strive to enhance our sustainability efforts. We believe, it is our responsibility to safeguard the environment and contribute positively to the communities we serve. Our proactive approach to ESG not only allows us to abide by regulatory standards but, also empowers us to create value for all our stakeholders and contribute to a greener and more sustainable world.

GreenInfinity Journey

1984

Mr. Rajesh Mittal starts operations with a saw mill

1988

Setting up 1st plywood unit in Tizit, Nagaland

1990

Incorporated as company under the name Mittal Laminated Pvt Ltd

1994

Transformed into public limited company - Mittal Laminated Limited

1996

Name changed Greenply Industries Limited

1997

Launch of Greenply brand (Flagship Premium Brand)

1997 (Contd.)

Plant at Gandhidham, Gujarat

1999

Decorative Veneer Manufacturing Unit at Bamanbore

2003

Plywood Manufacturing Unit at Pantnagar, Uttarakhand

2005

Amalgamation of worthy plywood, now Kriparamour factory

2007

Acquisition of two plywood units in Gujarat, now in Bamanbore Factory

2008

Implementation of SAP and launch of Optima Brand & flush doors

2014

Commenced production of Face Veneer in Myanmar

2015

Demerger of Decorative Business comprising of Laminates and allied products into Greenlam

2017

Face Veneer plant with capacity of 36,000 CBM has commenced production at Gabon in West Africa

2018

Production of Decorative Veneers starts at Bamanbore Plant

2019

Demerger of MDF Business and Pantnagar Plywood Unit into Greenpanel

2020

Introduction of E-Zero Range

2021

Foray into MDF segment

2023

Greenply's entry into future fittings with the launch of Greenply Sarnet Pvt. Ltd
